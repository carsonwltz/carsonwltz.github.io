About Projects
  • I believe websites should be simple, efficient, and effective for the end user. Too many websites nowadays have cluttered UIs and are bloated with trackers, ads, and other unnecessary scripts that compromise privacy and make poorer user experiences.
  • My goal is to make projects that provide better alternatives to popular websites that are ultimately easier to use for the end user.
  • I believe free and open source software—and open source as a whole—is the only way to create a better and more usable internet. The internet should be liberated, peer-to-peer, and controlled by the people, not a few select governments and companies.
